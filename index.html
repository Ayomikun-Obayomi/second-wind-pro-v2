Agency Statement

01 Agency Statement

Second Wind Pro was built on a singular premise: the landscape has changed, but the standard of elite representation has not.

We sit at the intersection of cultural influence and commercial execution—protecting the athlete, scaling their market value, and guiding their transition from high-school recruitment through the transfer portal and into the professional arena.

We exist to serve athletes who want more: control, compensation, and a long-term career. Selective by design.
