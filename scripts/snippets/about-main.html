Our story

02 How it started

Second Wind was born in the chaos of the 2024 transfer portal, when athletes with six-figure potential were left unrepresented and under-advised in the new NIL world.

The House v. NCAA settlement signaled a true pay-to-play era—and our founder left software sales to build a different kind of agency: trusted, not transactional. We advise athletes and universities with representation, consulting, and technology under one roof.
